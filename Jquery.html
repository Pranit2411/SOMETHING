Welcome to Pet-E-Care

Pet-e-Care is on a mission of ensuring each pet gets the best in healthcare in India, providing comprehensive medical, surgical, and dental care. Our veterinarian has many years’ experience treating pets. Our convenient clinic hours make it easier to schedule appointments that fit your busy schedule. Whether your pet is lounging in our boarding facility or recuperating in our care, we are committed to its safety and comfort. Whether your pets are a playful puppy, a cat entering the golden years, or a bird that isn’t feeling well, they will receive world-class care from the time they come into the hospital until the time they leave. Regular physical examinations and vaccinations are very important because we can help identify potential problems before they become more serious. We offer a wide variety of veterinary services for your treasured companion. Our benchmarks are high, our staff and hardware is first-rate, and we are enthusiastic about veterinary medicine. We anticipate meeting you and your pet!
